🚀 Quick Start Guide

1

Visit LinkedIn

Navigate to linkedin.com to see Canner in action.

2

Create Your First Response

Click the extension icon or select text and click the + button to save your first quick response.

3

Use Quick Responses

In any LinkedIn message box, look for the "💬 Quick Response" button or press Ctrl+Shift+L.

4

Manage Your Library

Click the extension icon to search, edit, and organize all your saved responses.
